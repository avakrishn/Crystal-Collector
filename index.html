Crystal Collector

Instructions

  • You will be given a random number to match at the start of the game.
  • There are four crystals below. The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.
  • By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of oints to your total score.
  • Each time the game starts, the game will generate a new random number to match and new values for each crystal.
  • You win the game by matching your total score to the random number, you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.
